About Me

Welcome my name is Kelsey Lyngdal. I'm a born a raised "Minnesotan from Plymouth, MN, but currently live in the Maple Grove area. My 5 year plan includes moving out of the mid-west and settling down in warmer weather. I'm looking forward to gaining more knowledge in the software development field!
